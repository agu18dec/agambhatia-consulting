Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about AI implementation consulting services.

How do I know if my startup is ready for AI implementation?

Every startup has different levels of AI readiness. Through an initial assessment, I'll evaluate your data infrastructure, technical capabilities, and business objectives to determine the most appropriate AI implementation strategy for your current stage and funding situation.

What industries do you specialize in?

I've worked with startups across e-commerce, fintech, healthtech, SaaS, and enterprise software. My approach is adaptable to different industry contexts, focusing on the underlying data and business processes that can benefit from AI enhancement to create defensible moats.

How long does a typical AI implementation project take?

Implementation timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. For startups, I focus on rapid prototyping with initial implementations in 2-4 weeks, followed by iterative improvements. More comprehensive solutions might require 2-3 months of development, always with an emphasis on showing early results.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, I offer flexible ongoing advisory services to ensure your AI systems continue to perform optimally as your startup scales. This can include regular performance reviews, troubleshooting support, and guidance on incorporating emerging technologies, all tailored to your growing business needs.
